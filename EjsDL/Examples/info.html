EJS digital library collection at http://fem.um.es/EjsDL/Examples

This collection of EJS models lists the examples contained in the EjsDL/Examples directory of http://fem.um.es.

EJS models in this collection are organized (alphabetically) by categories in the table of contents tree displayed in the left hand side of this display. Click on a subject folder icon to browse the available models or subcategories. The Download button retrieves the selected model from the digital library, installs it in your local EJS workspace, and then reads it.
